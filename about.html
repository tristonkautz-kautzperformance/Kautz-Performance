About Kautz Performance

Kautz Performance is a small coaching project focused on practical, realistic training for real athletes—people with school, jobs, and other responsibilities who still want to perform at a high level.

About Coach Kautz

I’m a teacher and track & field coach who’s spent a lot of time experimenting on myself—especially with vertical jump training, sprinting, and in-season performance. This site is where I organize training for athletes I work with and share ideas that might help others.

The goal isn’t perfection. The goal is steady, sustainable progress: jumping higher, running faster, and staying healthy enough to keep stacking good weeks.

What to expect as an athlete

  • Simple, clear training structure.
  • Honest feedback and realistic expectations.
  • A focus on long-term development over short-term hype.

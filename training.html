Training

This page describes the types of training I build for athletes. Full, detailed plans live in the Athlete Portal for athletes I’m currently working with.

Coaching focus

Vertical Jump Coaching

For athletes who want to jump higher, dunk, or improve explosive power for sports like basketball, volleyball, or track & field.

  • Jump practice built into the week
  • Strength work that supports jumping instead of fighting it
  • Clear structure for hard and easy days

Sprint & Speed Development

For athletes who want to run faster in short sprints or improve acceleration and top speed for field and court sports.

  • Acceleration and max-velocity sessions
  • Supportive lifting and plyometrics
  • Emphasis on staying healthy and consistent

In-Season Support

For athletes who are in the middle of a season and need help balancing competition, practice, and smart training.

  • Low-junk, high-value sessions
  • Adjustments based on meet schedule
  • Guidance on when to push and when to back off

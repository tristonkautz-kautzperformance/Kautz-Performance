What is Kautz Performance?

Kautz Performance is a small, focused coaching project built around simple, effective training for athletes who want to sprint faster, jump higher, and perform better in-season without burning out.

This site is the hub for current athletes I’m working with, plus a place to share articles and training thoughts that anyone can read.

For current athletes

If you’re currently working with me, your up-to-date training blocks and notes will live in the Athlete Portal. You’ll need the access code I gave you to view that page.

You’ll also find general resources here: warm-up routines, recovery guidelines, and explanations of how to read your training plan.
